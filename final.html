  • Project Description

    Monarchy Madness is a text-based roleplay game that allows you to govern an empire. Players will be given various situations to respond to each year. Try to make the best decisions for the empire!

    You can use your troops to conquer various provinces and gain wealth that will bring prosperity to your empire. You will also be tasked with keeping the morale of your people high. Watch out for devastating rebellions! As you conquer more provinces, your empire will expand. Your wealth will fluctuate depending on your decisions. The longer you live, the higher your winning score!
